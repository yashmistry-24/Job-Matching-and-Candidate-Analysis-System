Title: Data Analyst
Location: Remote
Job Type: Full-Time

Responsibilities:
Collect, organize, and analyze large datasets to identify trends, insights, and actionable recommendations.
Design and maintain dashboards, reports, and visualizations for cross-functional teams.
Perform data cleansing, transformation, and validation to ensure data accuracy and consistency.
Collaborate with business stakeholders to define and measure key performance indicators (KPIs).
Identify opportunities for automation and process improvement within data workflows.
Communicate findings and insights through reports and presentations to non-technical audiences.

Requirements:
Experience: 2+ years
Education: Bachelor's degree in Data Analytics, Statistics, Computer Science, or a related field.

Technical Skills:
Proficiency in SQL for data querying and manipulation.
Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or Looker).
Familiarity with Python or R for data analysis and scripting.
Strong understanding of Excel, including advanced formulas and pivot tables.

Other Skills:
Excellent problem-solving and analytical abilities.
Strong written and verbal communication skills to present findings effectively.
Ability to work independently and as part of a team in a fast-paced environment.

Preferred Qualifications:
Master's degree in a relevant field.
Experience with big data technologies (e.g., Hadoop, Spark).
Familiarity with cloud-based data platforms (AWS Redshift, Google BigQuery, or Azure Data Lake).
Knowledge of statistical methods and predictive modeling techniques.

Perks and Benefits:
Competitive salary with performance bonuses.
Flexible working hours and remote work opportunities.
Health insurance and wellness programs.
Professional development and learning reimbursements.
